# Commercial Licensing

Hashsite offers two paid paths for users who do not qualify for the free public license:

1. **Hashsite Developer Welcome Kit** — a $999, 3-year, limited commercial license for a single legal entity, small team, and limited deployment.
2. **Negotiated Commercial License** — for broader deployment, affiliate use, OEM rights, source redistribution rights, government-wide or enterprise-wide rollout, support, or custom terms.

## Who needs a paid license

A paid commercial license is required before any use by:

- governments
- militaries
- intelligence agencies
- law-enforcement agencies
- public-sector bodies
- contractors acting on their behalf
- large commercial enterprises

A **Large Commercial User** includes a for-profit entity that, alone or with its affiliates, has more than 250 employees, more than $50M annual revenue, is publicly traded, or is majority-owned by such an entity.

## What the paid path covers

A paid commercial license can cover:

- evaluation
- procurement review
- internal testing
- prototype development
- limited or broad production deployment
- internal modification
- integration into products and services
- support and update terms
- negotiated scope beyond the Welcome Kit limits

## Welcome Kit vs negotiated license

Use the **Welcome Kit** if you need:

- one legal entity
- up to 5 named developers
- one product, service, or internal program
- limited production use
- a fixed 3-year term
- a low-friction path priced at **$999**

Use a **negotiated commercial license** if you need:

- more than 5 named developers
- multiple products or programs
- affiliate or subsidiary use
- enterprise-wide or government-wide deployment
- OEM, reseller, white-label, or sublicense rights
- public redistribution of modified source code
- contractual support, warranties, indemnities, or procurement paperwork
