# Licensing

Hashsite is **public-source**, not OSI open source.

## Free tier

Hashsite is free for **Free-Tier Users**: individuals, nonprofits, hobbyists, researchers, and smaller private-sector users that are **not**:

- governments or public-sector entities
- military, intelligence, law-enforcement, or related bodies
- contractors acting on behalf of the above
- large commercial enterprises

Free-Tier Users may use Hashsite in production, make private internal modifications, and ship products or services that incorporate **unmodified** portions of the Hashsite reference implementation, with attribution, under the [LICENSE](LICENSE).

## Paid tier

A paid commercial license is required for:

- governments
- militaries
- intelligence agencies
- law-enforcement agencies
- public-sector bodies
- contractors acting on their behalf
- large commercial enterprises

A **Large Commercial User** includes a for-profit entity that, alone or with its affiliates, has more than 250 employees, more than $50M annual revenue, is publicly traded, or is majority-owned by such an entity.

## Developer Welcome Kit

For developers inside a government, military, or large enterprise who need a quick, lawful on-ramp, Hashsite offers a **Developer Welcome Kit**:

- **$999**
- **3-year term**
- **up to 5 named developers**
- **one legal entity**
- **one product, service, or internal program**
- **limited production use**

Read the full terms in [WELCOME-KIT.md](WELCOME-KIT.md).


## Public-interest waivers

For selected mission-aligned projects that would otherwise fall into the paid buckets, Hashsite may offer a no-cost or reduced-cost license.

Typical examples include conservation and parks, humanitarian demining, search and rescue, accessibility, education, and public-interest research.

See [PUBLIC-INTEREST-WAIVER.md](PUBLIC-INTEREST-WAIVER.md) for the request policy.

## Broader commercial terms

For broader deployment, OEM use, affiliate use, source redistribution rights, or organization-wide rollout, see [COMMERCIAL-LICENSE.md](COMMERCIAL-LICENSE.md) or contact **licensing@hashsite.org**.
