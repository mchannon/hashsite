# Public-Interest Fee Waiver Policy

Hashsite may offer a **no-cost or reduced-cost commercial license** for selected projects that would otherwise fall into the paid licensing buckets.

This is meant to reduce friction for mission-aligned work without making governments, militaries, major contractors, or large corporations free by default.

## Typical candidates

A fee waiver or reduced-fee license may be considered for projects such as:

- conservation and parks
- humanitarian demining
- search and rescue
- disability and accessibility tools
- education and public-interest research
- libraries, museums, archives, and cultural preservation
- non-profit field operations with limited budgets
- pilot projects with clear public benefit and limited deployment scope

## Typical non-candidates

A fee waiver is usually **not** the default path for:

- broad agency-wide deployment
- broad military or defense deployment
- Fortune 500 or similar large-enterprise rollout
- commercial resale or OEM use
- projects with large existing procurement budgets
- requests that are mainly about avoiding a standard paid license

## What to send

To request a waiver or reduced-fee license, email **licensing@hashsite.org** with:

- legal entity name
- project name
- short mission description
- why Hashsite helps
- expected user count or deployment scope
- timeline
- whether the use is a pilot, internal tool, or production deployment
- whether the entity is government, contractor, nonprofit, or private company

## Possible outcomes

Hashsite may, in its sole discretion, offer:

- a no-cost pilot license
- a reduced-fee Welcome Kit
- a reduced-fee negotiated license
- a time-limited waiver
- a scope-limited waiver
- or no waiver

A waiver, if granted, applies only to the named entity, named project, and approved scope.

It is not transferable, not sublicensable, and does not create any right to future waivers.
